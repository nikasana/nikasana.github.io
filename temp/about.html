Our Mission



The Academy of Future of Georgia was established on August 19, 2015.

The organization's mission is to create modern and successful Georgia.

We believe that the Georgian society needs a lot of fundamental changes in order to transform Georgia into a modern, successful state. Such changes in the existing reality are unimaginable without the young generation. That's why, the goal of the "Future Academy of Georgia" is to create a critical mass in a new generation that will be able to make these changes.
